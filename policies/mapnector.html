Expanding on Location Data

If you click on the main send location checkbox, you will be prompted to grant Mapnector permission to send location data. When this checkbox is on, and you turn on checkbox to send location data so a specific group, your precise location information will be sent, and other members of that specific group will be able to see you on the map.

Once you turn off the main checkbox, or the one to send location data to that specific group, we immediatly delete the location information from our servers and noeone can access your location data anymore. Also, once you pause the app, location data is deleted too, so if you are interested in sharing your location, make sure you remain inside Mapnector.

We understand the sensitivity of this information. We ensure you that we implemented various mechanisms to ensure that we erase the data as soon as you are not interested in sharing it anymore with other group members.





Your Rights And Options

Push Notifications

As Mapnector can listen to group chats in background if you configure it to do so, we notify you about new messages using push notifications.

You can manage the shown notifications to you using notification channels. You may also disable them entirely.

Pro tip: as those are the only notifications of Mapnector just turn off chat listeners.

Location Data

As described above, you can share your location information with other group members.

You may also use Mapnector just to communicate with other people, without ever giving us location permission. It is an optional feature that you can turn on if you want.
