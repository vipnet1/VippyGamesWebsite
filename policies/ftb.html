In a Nutshell

For The Battle is an online game. We use servers to connect players together, different services to backup your data, your in game stats and enhance game experience. We use some third party providers for this task.

Also, as there are in game monetization systems, data relevant to in app purchases, ads may be stored or sent.

And yet, we assure you that your data is in good hands.
We, as Vippy Games, use your data only to:
- Enable in game features & enhance your experience
- Implement monetization systems
- Debug in game errors & fix issues

Regarding other third party providers, all of them are large and reputable entities.
You may view their usage of your data in the links provided in this document.

Data We Store Locally On Your Device

Mostly data relevant to the game. Local settings, levels progress tracking, your skins, abilities, level, amount of coins etc.

Some authentication related data might be stored locally. For example anonymous sign in id.





Data Collected from your device

Google Play Store

We collect from you the data that is collected by default from apps published on Google Play Store.

This data may include ANRs, crashes, startup times and other performance and errors related data. We use this data ONLY to fix problems and make the game better.

In addition, we use in app purchases, so relevant data regarding transactions history and spending on in app products will be collected as well.

Google Play Games

We use it to enhance your experience. We might collect your friend list, achievements, email address, nickname, in game progress and other Play Games relevant data.

Unity

As we use it as a provider to authenticate you, store your in game progress, and might use other Unity Gaming Services features, relevant data will be saved on Unity's servers.

In addition to that, We collect from you the data that is collected by default from apps made by the Unity game engine.

Photon Engine

Used as a server to connect players to the game. Different players identifiers, nicknames, ip addresses among the data that might be collected.

Ads

Your advertisement id might be sent from the device to show you personalized ads. We may use Google or Unity as ads provider.
