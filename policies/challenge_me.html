Challenge Me Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 15th, 2025



Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding your personal data or how we store it - we encourage you to contact us using the email address provided below.





In a Nutshell

Challenge Me is an offline game. We do not request from you, store, collect or send to anyone any personal, identifying or sensitive data that we know of.

And yet, this game was made using Unity game engine and published on Google Play Store. Those services may use your data.

Data We Store Locally On Your Device

None that might interest you. Local settings, levels progress tracking etc.
Data that is required for the normal functioning of the game.





Data Collected from your device

Google Play Store

We collect from you the data that is collected by default from apps published on Google Play Store.

This data may include ANRs, crashes, startup times and other performance and errors related data. We use this data ONLY to fix problems and make the game better.

Of course if you are interested in retrieving or deleting this data, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Unity

We collect from you the data that is collected by default from apps made by the Unity game engine.
